Register an account

Register an account to manage your discount, save product comparisons, and get personalised advice and learning.

Before you start

To create an account, you must be:

  • aged 18 or over
  • a PAYE employee of the business
  • authorised by the business to agree to the terms and conditions

What you’ll need

To register your account, you’ll need to tell us:

  • your business’ Companies House or Financial Conduct Authority (FCA) Mutuals Public Register number
  • your name
  • your role in the business
  • your contact details

Where can I find my Companies House or FCA Mutuals Public Register number?

Companies House

This is also known as your company registration number. It can be found on your certificate of incorporation. You can also search the Companies House register.

Financial Conduct Authority (FCA) Mutuals Public Register

The FCA Mutuals Public Register is a public record of mutual societies registered by the Financial Conduct Authority. You can find this register number on the Financial Conduct Authority Mutuals Public Register.
